What is Stocksmart ML?

The corporate world is experiencing a transformative shift with increasing emphasis on diversity and inclusion. These shifts come with new factors that can influence business operations, regulatory environment, and ultimately, financial performance.

Stocksmart ML aims to use machine learning to find a new investment strategy. We study the impact of multiple factors from 2015-2023 against the individual stocks in the 2024 S&P 500 stocks.

We look at:

  • CEO Factors
  • External Factors
  • Company Factors

What is the S&P 500 Index?



  • Index comprised of 500 large companies

  • Industry benchmark that represents US stock market

  • Composition is weighted by market cap

Modeling Methods


Unsupervised


    ML method:
    • Elbow Method
    • Clusters using KMeans

    Output:
    • Visualized clusters

Supervised: Classification


    ML method:
    • KNN
    • ExtraTrees
    • Random Forest
    • Bagging Classifier
    Output:
    • Visualized clusters
    • Classification Report
    • Feature Importances

Supervised: Neural Network


    ML method:
    • Neural network with 2-4 layers
    • Nodes ranging from 10-256
    • Epochs from 100-200

    Output:
    • Accuracy Scores
    • nn_model to test on small set of new stocks
